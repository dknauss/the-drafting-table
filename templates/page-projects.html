2024-07 / Residential / Madison, WI

Meadow House

A single-story residence set into a gentle hillside outside Madison. Board-formed concrete, local limestone, and a cantilevered roof that reaches toward the oak grove to the west.

The plan unfolds as a gentle arc, following the contour of the land. Each room opens to its own garden view through floor-to-ceiling glazing. The concrete bearing walls, cast with local sand aggregate, carry the color of the surrounding earth.

Area

2,400 sq ft

Status

Under Construction

Materials

Concrete, limestone, oak

Lakeside Assembly Hall

Orchard Reading Pavilion

River Workshop Studios

2023-05 / Spiritual / Mineral Point, WI

Woodland Meditation Chapel

A place of silence in a birch clearing. The folded-plate concrete roof channels rainwater into a reflecting pool. Floor-to-ceiling glass dissolves the wall between worship and forest. Seating for twelve.

Every great architect is necessarily a great poet. They must be a great original interpreter of their time, their day, their age.
